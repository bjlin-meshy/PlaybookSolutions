# Rapid Prototyping (Greyboxing) / 快速原型制作

## 基本信息
- **行业**: Gaming / 游戏
- **标签**: Core Workflow / 核心工作流
- **HTML 文件**: `cases/case-gaming-prototype.html`

---

## 痛点 / Pain Points

### English
- Level designers use ugly greyboxes that fail to convey atmosphere
- Waste time modeling placeholder assets that get deleted
- Hard to get stakeholder buy-in with low-fidelity demos
- Purchasing asset packs is expensive and generic

### 中文
- 关卡设计师使用丑陋灰盒无法传达氛围
- 浪费时间建模最终被删除的占位资产
- 低保真 Demo 难以说服决策方
- 购买资产包昂贵且通用

---

## 解决方案 / Meshy Solution

### English
- Instant generation of textured props for immersive whiteboxing
- Generate 50+ props in hours to populate scenes
- Validate gameplay feel immediately with visuals
- Zero asset pack costs

### 中文
- 瞬间生成带纹理道具用于沉浸式白盒
- 几小时内生成 50+ 道具填充场景
- 用视觉即时验证玩法感受
- 零资产包成本
