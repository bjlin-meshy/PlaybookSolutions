## 痛点 / Pain Points

### English
- CAD tools (SketchUp) fail at natural cloth wrinkles
- Scenes look stiff and "fake" without organic details
- Simulating cloth physics is too technical/slow
- Manual modeling of bedding/curtains is time-consuming

### 中文
- CAD 工具（SketchUp）难以处理自然布料褶皱
- 缺少有机细节场景显得生硬"假"
- 模拟布料物理太技术化/慢
- 手动建模床品/窗帘耗时

---

## 解决方案 / Meshy Solution

### English
- Text-to-Softbody generates natural, messy cloth
- Add "lived-in" feel (messy bed, curtains) instantly
- No physics simulation required
- Fast staging with instant atmosphere

### 中文
- 文本转软体生成自然、凌乱布料
- 即时添加"生活感"（凌乱床铺、窗帘）
- 无需物理模拟
- 快速布置即时氛围
