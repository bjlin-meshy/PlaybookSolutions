## 痛点 / Pain Points

### English
- Physical CMF prototyping is extremely expensive
- Waiting for Keyshot rendering to test simple colorways
- Hard to visualize material combinations on white models
- Traditional prototyping cycle is weeks

### 中文
- 物理 CMF 打样极其昂贵
- 等待 Keyshot 渲染测试简单配色
- 白模上难以想象材质组合
- 传统打样周期需要数周

---

## 解决方案 / Meshy Solution

### English
- Apply AI texturing to white models instantly
- Test 50+ material combos (Wood/Metal/Plastic) in 1 hour
- No UV unwrapping required for quick visualization
- Virtual verification at $0 prototype cost

### 中文
- 对白模瞬间应用 AI 纹理
- 1 小时内测试 50+ 材质组合（木/金属/塑料）
- 无需 UV 拆解即可快速可视化
- 虚拟验证零打样成本

---

## 核心指标 / Key Metrics

| 指标 | 数值 | 说明 |
