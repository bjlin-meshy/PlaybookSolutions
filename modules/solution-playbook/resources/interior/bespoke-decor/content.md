# Bespoke Decor / 定制装饰品

## 基本信息
- **行业**: Interior Design / 室内设计
- **标签**: Reuse / 可复用
- **HTML 文件**: `cases/case-interior-decor.html`

---

## 痛点 / Pain Points

### English
- Luxury projects need unique art, but libraries are generic
- Custom modeling for a vase/statue is over-budget
- Hard to demonstrate unique taste with stock assets
- Client expectations for bespoke items are high

### 中文
- 豪华项目需要独特艺术品，但素材库太通用
- 为花瓶/雕塑定制建模超预算
- 用库存资产难以展示独特品味
- 客户对定制品期望高

---

## 解决方案 / Meshy Solution

### English
- Generate one-of-a-kind art based on mood boards
- Create unique sculptures/vases in minutes
- Visualize "Bespoke" without the cost
- Infinite library of unique assets

### 中文
- 根据情绪板生成独一无二艺术品
- 几分钟内创建独特雕塑/花瓶
- 无成本可视化"定制"效果
- 无限独特资产库

---

## 核心指标 / Key Metrics

| 指标 | 数值 | 说明 |
