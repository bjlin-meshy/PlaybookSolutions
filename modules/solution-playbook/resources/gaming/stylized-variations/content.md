## 痛点 / Pain Points

### English
- Creating 10+ variants (fire, ice, ruined) is repetitive labor
- Outsourcing leads to fragmented art styles
- Iteration cycles for "reskinning" take days
- Manual modeling for each variant is expensive

### 中文
- 制作 10+ 种变体（火、冰、废墟）是重复劳动
- 外包导致风格不统一
- 换皮迭代周期需要数天
- 为每个变体手动建模成本高

---

## 解决方案 / Meshy Solution

### English
- Generate infinite variants from one master model
- Local Refine fixes details without breaking the base
- 100% style consistency via Reference Image control
- 10+ sets in 1 day possible

### 中文
- 基于主模型无限生成变体
- 局部重绘精修细节不影响底座
- 通过参考图控制保证 100% 风格统一
- 1 天可产出 10+ 套变体
