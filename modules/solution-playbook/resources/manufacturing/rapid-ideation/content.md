- **标签**: Reuse / 可复用

---

## 痛点 / Pain Points

### English
- Sketching is fast, but converting to 3D for volume check is slow
- Designers struggle to communicate volume with 2D drawings
- Waiting for CAD modelers creates bottlenecks
- Days of CAD work for initial form validation

### 中文
- 草图快速，但转 3D 验证体积慢
- 设计师难以用 2D 图纸传达体积感
- 等待 CAD 建模师造成瓶颈
- 初始形态验证需要数天 CAD 工作

---

## 解决方案 / Meshy Solution

### English
- Sketch-to-3D visualization for instant form validation
- Check volume and proportions in 3D immediately
- Iterate forms rapidly before committing to CAD
- 30-minute validation vs days of CAD

### 中文
- 草图转 3D 可视化实现即时形态验证
- 立即在 3D 中检查体积和比例
- 在进入 CAD 前快速迭代形态
- 30 分钟验证 vs 数天 CAD
