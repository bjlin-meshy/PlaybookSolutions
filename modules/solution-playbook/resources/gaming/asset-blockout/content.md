## 痛点 / Pain Points

### English
- Artists waste days on basic sculpting (blockout) before styling
- Senior talent is stuck doing "dirty work" instead of polishing
- 2D-to-3D interpretation errors cause rework
- Manual blockout takes 2-4 days per asset

### 中文
- 建模师在起型阶段浪费大量时间
- 资深人才被困在"脏活累活"而非精修
- 2D 转 3D 理解偏差导致返工
- 手动起型每资产需要 2-4 天

---

## 解决方案 / Meshy Solution

### English
- AI generates 80% complete high-poly bases in minutes
- Skip blockout, go straight to ZBrush refinement
- Instant volume visualization from concept art
- Same-day progression to detail work

### 中文
- AI 几分钟生成 80% 完成度高模底座
- 跳过起型，直接进入 ZBrush 精修
- 概念图即时体积可视化
- 当天即可进入细节工作
