# Rapid Virtual Staging / 快速虚拟软装

## 基本信息
- **行业**: Interior Design / 室内设计
- **标签**: Reuse / 可复用
- **HTML 文件**: `cases/case-interior-staging.html`

---

## 痛点 / Pain Points

### English
- Empty rooms are hard to sell in Real Estate
- Modeling furniture just for staging is too expensive
- Purchasing staging furniture assets adds up
- Time pressure for property listings

### 中文
- 空房难以在房地产市场销售
- 仅为布置建模家具太贵
- 购买布置家具资产成本累积
- 房源上架时间压力

---

## 解决方案 / Meshy Solution

### English
- Quickly fill scanned rooms (LiDAR) with AI furniture
- Does not need perfect geometry, just visual volume
- Stage a whole house in an afternoon
- Low cost, high sales impact

### 中文
- 快速用 AI 家具填充扫描房间（LiDAR）
- 不需要完美几何，只需视觉体积
- 一下午布置整套房源
- 低成本高销售影响

---

| 指标 | 数值 | 说明 |
