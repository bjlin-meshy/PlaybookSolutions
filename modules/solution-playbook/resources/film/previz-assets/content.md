# Cinematic Previz Assets / 影视预演资产

## 基本信息
- **行业**: Film & Animation / 影视与动画
- **标签**: Reuse / 可复用
- **HTML 文件**: `cases/case-film-previz.html`

---

## 痛点 / Pain Points

### English
- Directors struggle to visualize shots with just grey cubes
- Waiting for art dept assets slows down Layout/Previz
- Storyboards lack spatial depth information
- High cost for throwaway previz assets

### 中文
- 导演难以通过灰色方块想象镜头画面
- 等待美术部门资产拖慢预演进度
- 故事板缺乏空间深度信息
- 一次性预演资产成本高

---

## 解决方案 / Meshy Solution

### English
- Fill scenes with textured props in under 5 minutes
- Rapidly iterate set designs during storyboarding
- Better communication between Director and DoP
- Zero asset cost for previz phase

### 中文
- 5 分钟内用带纹理道具填充场景
- 在故事板阶段快速迭代场景设计
- 导演与摄影指导沟通更顺畅
- 预演阶段零资产成本
